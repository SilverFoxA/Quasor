Quasor Rocketry LLP is an education, training and technology based company which has a sole purpose of integrating real world ideas with the latest in technology. By developing an active interest towards space-science and technology, we aim to lay a strong foundation for the development of Aerospace industry in our country. Quasor Rocketry LLP is first of its kind in the country that aims to organise Model Rocketry across various parts of India. We are a constant facilitator in the scientific and technical education of students of all levels by providing them classroom education, seminars, workshops, other hands on resources through wide variety of hardware, software and web based learning. We extend our support and technical expertise to the young minds for the sake of developing science in the nation.
